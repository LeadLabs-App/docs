## Get started in three steps

### Step 1: Install our Chrome Extension

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Click to Install the Extension" icon="chrome">
    [Click here](https://leadlabs.app/install) to install the extension. It will automatically redirect you to the Chrome Store.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Sign Up (Or Sign In)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Google Sign Up" icon="google">
    There are two ways of signing up for LeadLabs. The simplest method is to sign in with your Google personal email or Google Workspace account.

    ![Signup SS Pn](/images/SignupSS.png)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Manual SIgn Up" icon="right-to-bracket">
    You can manually enter your name and email (if you do not have a Google email) and proceed to sign up by following the steps as shown on the sign up screen.

    <Warning>
      For verification purposes, LeadLabs will send you a One Time Sign-Up Code to the above email address. Make sure you have access to it during the process.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Calibrate

<Accordion title="Calibrate your AI Companion" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
  1. Visit your LinkedIn profile after signing in to LeadLabs, then our AI companion will automatically prompt you to calibrate the AI companion by clicking on "Set Up My AI Assistant".

     <Check>
       [Click here to visit your LinkedIn Profile.](https://www.linkedin.com/in/me/)
     </Check>
     ![Calibrate Pn](/images/Calibrate.png)
  2. Follow the on screen instructions and you're all set to go\!
</Accordion>
